About Logan

Logan Bashore (born 2007) is a Christian percussionist, composer, and educator from Florida. He is a percussion instructor for the East Lake Silver Sound and is contracted as a performing member of Infinity 3 Percussion for their 2026 season. He credits his success to the Lord and the support of his peers.

An alumnus of the Tarpon Springs Leadership Conservatory for the Arts, Logan’s accolades include multiple Superior ratings at FBA Solo & Ensemble, highlighted by a Superior with Distinction at State for his performance of Undertale Variations in 2025, as well as performing in the 2022 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade.

Throughout his music career, Logan has composed works spanning solo and ensemble percussion performance, including a commission from percussion performer and influencer Eric “EMCProductions” Carr. He also contributed as a multi-instrumentalist to Luke Pickman’s Undertale 10th Anniversary project, a YouTube collaboration featuring 600+ musicians.

Logan has studied under several influential educators in the activity, including Jeff Ausdemore, Ivan Trevino, Andrea Venet, Josh Brickey, Eliana Yamouni, Oliver DeLotto, Dan Hartung, AJ Vega, Courtney Thompson, Howard Weinstein, Tim Sexton, and Kevin Ford.
