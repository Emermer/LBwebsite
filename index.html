About Logan

Logan Bashore (b. 2007) is a Christian percussionist, composer, and educator from Florida. He serves as a Percussion Instructor for the East Lake High School Band and is currently studying at St. Petersburg College.

Logan began his music journey on piano at a young age and later developed his passion for percussion after being exposed to the marching arts. He is an alumnus of the Tarpon Springs Leadership Conservatory for the Arts (TSLCA) and was a performing member of Infinity 3 Percussion in 2026. His time in the activity led him to performing nationally at events such as BOA Grand Nationals, WGI World Championships, and the 2022 Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade.

Logan has been committed to writing for ensembles both nationally and internationally. His work has been commissioned across the United States and the Philippines, including a commission from percussion performer and influencer EMCProductions. He was also a featured performer in Luke Pickman’s 2025 Worldwide Orchestra, a YouTube collaboration featuring 600+ musicians. He also earned consistent superior ratings on snare drum and marimba through participation in FBA Solo & Ensemble, including a Superior with Distinction at State for his performance of Undertale Variations in 2025.

Logan was recognized at TSLCA through awards including the Commitment to Excellence Scholarship (Russ & Lu Tardiff) in 2022, the Percussion Director’s Award in 2024, and the Most Outstanding Senior Percussionist Award in 2025. He was also recognized with the Infinity 3 Performer of the Year award in 2026.
