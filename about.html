About Logan Bashore

Logan Bashore is a percussionist and composer from Florida, currently a senior at Tarpon Springs High School. He is a member of the Tarpon Springs Leadership Conservatory for the Arts, where he has served as the center snare and drumline section leader for the Tarpon Springs Outdoor Performance Ensemble. Logan is also an active participant and section leader in the TSLCA Wind Ensemble and has previously performed with the TSLCA Wind Symphony, as well as with the TSLCA Jazz Ensemble as a pianist. His diverse musical background and leadership roles showcase his commitment and passion for the arts.

In addition to his performance experience, Logan is passionate about the marching arts and works as a freelance percussion composer and arranger on Fiverr. He aspires to write for high school programs and drum corps, with plans to perform with a corps in the future.
