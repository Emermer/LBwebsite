About Logan Bashore

Logan Bashore is a percussionist, composer, and arranger from Florida. He began his musical journey on the piano at a young age but later shifted his focus to percussion after being exposed to the marching arts. Currently a senior at Tarpon Springs High School, Logan is a member of the Tarpon Springs Leadership Conservatory for the Arts (TSLCA). He previously served as drumline section leader and center snare for the Tarpon Springs Outdoor Performance Ensemble. He is also an active member of the TSLCA Wind Ensemble and has previously performed with the TSLCA Wind Symphony and the TSLCA Jazz Ensemble as a pianist. His diverse musical background and leadership roles reflect his dedication and passion for the arts.

In addition to his performance experience, Logan is passionate about the marching arts and works as a freelance percussion composer and arranger on Fiverr. He aspires to write for high school programs and drum corps, with plans to perform with a corps in the future.
