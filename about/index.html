About Logan

Logan Bashore is a percussionist, composer, and arranger from Florida. He began his musical journey on piano at a young age but later shifted his focus to percussion after discovering the marching arts. Currently a senior at Tarpon Springs High School, Logan is a member of the Tarpon Springs Leadership Conservatory for the Arts (TSLCA), where he has previously served as drumline section leader and center snare for the Tarpon Springs Outdoor Performance Ensemble. He is also an active member of the TSLCA Wind Ensemble and has performed with both the TSLCA Wind Symphony and the TSLCA Jazz Ensemble as a pianist. His diverse musical background and leadership roles reflect his dedication and passion for the arts.

Logan’s dedication to his craft has led to a number of accomplishments throughout his time in the activity. With the Tarpon Springs Outdoor Performance Ensemble, Logan has competed nationally at the BOA Grand National Championships held at Lucas Oil Stadium in both 2022 and 2024, where the ensemble earned finalist placements in both years. Throughout his high school career, Logan earned superior ratings at FBA Solo and Ensemble all four years, showcasing his growth and dedication as a performer. He received a superior rating for his performance of Backseat Freestyle—a grade 7 marching snare solo—in 2024, and a superior rating for Undertale Variations, a grade 5 four-mallet marimba solo, in 2025. Also in 2025, he achieved a superior with distinction rating at State Solo and Ensemble for Undertale Variations, a significant recognition of his skill and performance. Within TSLCA, Logan has been recognized with several awards, including Most Improved Freshman (2021–2022), Most Improved Junior (2023–2024), the Percussion Director’s Award (2023–2024), and the Russ & Lu Tardiff Commitment to Excellence Scholarship (2021–2022). These accomplishments highlight his growth, versatility, and passion for music, while also showcasing his dedication to the field.

Building on those experiences, Logan continues to pursue his passion for the marching arts beyond performance. He works as a freelance percussion composer and arranger on Fiverr and aspires to write for high school programs and drum corps. He also intends to perform with a corps in the near future while continuing to grow as both a writer and performer in the activity.
