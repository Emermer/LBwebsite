About Logan

Logan Bashore is a Christian percussionist, composer, and arranger from Florida. He’s currently a percussion instructor for the East Lake High School Silver Sound Marching Band, additionally working as a freelance marching percussion composer & arranger on Fiverr. He credits his success to the Lord and the support of his peers.

Logan is an alumnus of the Tarpon Springs High School Leadership Conservatory for the Arts (TSLCA), where he has served as drumline section leader, wind ensemble section leader, and center snare. Within his time of membership, he was recognized with the Most Outstanding Senior Percussionist Award (‘25), Director's Award – Percussion (‘24), Most Improved Junior & Freshman Percussionist Awards (‘24, ‘22), and the Russ & Lu Tardiff Commitment to Excellence Scholarship (‘22).

Logan has commissioned numerous percussion ensemble pieces throughout his composing career, most notably writing a show movement for percussion performer and influencer Eric Carr, better known as EMCProductions. He aspires to write and teach for DCI, WGI, and scholastic groups across the country, with plans to march with a corps in the near future.

Among Logan’s many highlights as a member of TSLCA were performing at the BOA Grand National Championships in 2022 and 2024, as well as marching in the 96th Annual Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade in 2022. He earned consistent superior ratings for FBA’s Solo & Ensemble contest, most honorably receiving a superior with distinction rating at State S&E for his performance of Undertale Variations in 2025.
