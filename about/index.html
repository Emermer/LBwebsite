About Logan

Logan Bashore is a Christian percussionist, composer, and educator from Florida. He works commissioning music for marching percussion on Fiverr, and recently finished a season with the East Lake Silver Sound as a percussion instructor in 2025. He is contracted as a performing member of Infinity 3 Percussion for their 2026 season. He credits his success to the Lord and the support of his peers.

Logan is an alumnus of the Tarpon Springs Leadership Conservatory for the Arts, where he served as the drumline section leader, the wind ensemble percussion section leader, and center snare. Within his time of membership, he was recognized with the Most Outstanding Senior Percussionist Award (‘25), Director's Award – Percussion (‘24), Most Improved Junior & Freshman Percussionist Awards (‘24, ‘22), and the Russ & Lu Tardiff Commitment to Excellence Scholarship (‘22).

Throughout his compositional career, Logan has composed works spanning percussion ensemble and solo percussion performance, including a notable commission from percussion performer and influencer Eric “EMCProductions” Carr. As a performer, he earned consistent Superior ratings at the FBA Solo & Ensemble Festival, including a Superior with Distinction at State for Undertale Variations in 2025, and was a four-year member of the PCMEA All-County Concert Band.

Logan has studied under several influential educators in the activity, including Jeff Ausdemore, Ivan Trevino, Andrea Venet, Josh Brickey, Eliana Yamouni, Oliver DeLotto, Dan Hartung, AJ Vega, Courtney Thompson, Howard Weinstein, Tim Sexton, and Kevin Ford.
